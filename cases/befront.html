Befront

Landing Page
About the Case

Task: Show that the training is structured and serious while still providing an easy way to start for both beginners and experienced designers who need practice.

Solution: A friendly design with a light, almost playful style and purple and yellow accents. The core idea is that practice matters more than theory.

Result: The website feels like an extension of the product itself: once you enter the platform, you already understand how the learning experience is organized. The page attracts both beginners who are unsure about starting and experienced designers who need practice.
